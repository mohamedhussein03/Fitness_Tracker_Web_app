Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the purpose of Track Pal?
The fitness tracker is designed to help you monitor your physical activity, track your progress, and achieve your fitness goals.
How do I set up my fitness tracker?
Sign up and create an account, fill in your body preferences and view your index, select your daily food to calculate the calories.
